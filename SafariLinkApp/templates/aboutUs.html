About SafariLink Travelers

Background

Transportation within Kenya, a nation marked by diverse landscapes and vibrant urban centers, has witnessed a transformative shift with the advent of technology. The Travel Management System (TMS) emerges as a crucial player in this evolution, aiming to redefine the experience of booking and managing vehicle travel for passengers across the country.

Purpose

The purpose of the Travel Management System is to offer a convenient and secure platform for passengers to book and pay for travel services. Additionally, the system includes features such as real-time route optimization, accident alerts, and accurate arrival time estimations.

Problem Statement

The travel industry faces challenges related to optimizing routes, addressing real-time traffic disruptions, and providing a secure and efficient booking process. Traditional methods often lack the integration of technological advancements, leading to inconveniences for both passengers and drivers. Additionally, ensuring passenger safety in unforeseen circumstances, such as accidents or traffic jams, is a critical concern that requires a systematic solution.
